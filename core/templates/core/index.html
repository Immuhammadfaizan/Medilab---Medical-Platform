Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our medical services, appointments, and patient care.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our online booking system on the website, by calling our reception, or by visiting the hospital in person.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we accept walk-in patients; however, priority is given to those with scheduled appointments. Emergency cases are attended to immediately.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance providers. Please check with our front desk or your insurance company for coverage details.

What should I bring for my first appointment?

Please bring your ID card, insurance details (if applicable), previous medical records, and a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Do you offer telemedicine consultations?

Yes, we offer telemedicine services for certain medical conditions. You can schedule a virtual consultation through our website or by calling our reception.

What are your hospital's visiting hours?

Our visiting hours are from 9 AM to 8 PM daily. However, ICU and special care units may have restricted visiting times.

How do I get my medical test results?

Test results are available through our patient portal, via email, or by visiting the hospital. Some reports may take 24-48 hours for processing.
